ReviewGuard Reply Support

ReviewGuard Reply is a beta Chrome extension for drafting safer Google review replies. It does not auto-post replies; users review and publish manually in Google.

For help, bug reports, or beta feedback, email contact@linqen.co.kr.

Useful details to include:

  • Chrome version
  • Whether you were using Google Business Profile or Google Search/Maps review management
  • A short description of what you expected and what happened
